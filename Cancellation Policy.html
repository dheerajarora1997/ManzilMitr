Cancellation Policy

The initial booking amount is non-refundable in the event of any cancellation.

  • For cancellations made 30 days or more before the trip's start date: 50% of the total trip cost will be retained as cancellation fees.
  • For cancellations made 15–30 days before the trip's start date: 75% of the total trip cost will be charged as cancellation fees.
  • For cancellations made within 0–15 days of the trip's start date: 100% of the total trip cost will be charged as cancellation fees.

In the event of unforeseen weather conditions or government restrictions: certain activities may need to be canceled. While the operator will make every effort to offer an alternative activity, no refunds will be provided in such cases.
